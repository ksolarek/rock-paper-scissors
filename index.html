ROCK PAPER SCISSORS

How to play?

The game is played until one player reaches 3 points. Click Rock, Paper, or Scissors to make your choice. The rules are simple:
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
  • If both players choose the same, the round is a draw.
Your score and the computer’s score are shown after each round. First to 3 points wins the game! Let's start!
